# Research Index

This folder contains the evidentiary foundation for Radical Moderate's product and strategy decisions. Each trend document follows a standard format: strategic relevance, core finding, sourced evidence, and caveats.

## Source Quality Standards

- **Tier 1** — Peer-reviewed journal articles (PNAS, Science, Nature, Econometrica, etc.)
- **Tier 2** — Major institutional reports (Reuters Institute, Edelman, Pew, etc.)
- **Tier 3** — Credible secondary coverage of Tier 1/2 findings
- **Avoid** — Opinion pieces, non-peer-reviewed blogs, self-reported company data
